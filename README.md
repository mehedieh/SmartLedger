## ✨ Overview

SmartLedger is a **production-grade, offline-first Business Management System** that combines inventory management, point of sale (POS), accounting, customer management, supplier management, financial reporting, and business analytics into one intuitive Android application.

Built with **Clean Architecture**, **MVVM**, and **Jetpack Compose**, SmartLedger emphasizes speed, reliability, security, and a premium user experience inspired by modern mobile design.

Unlike cloud-dependent business software, SmartLedger is designed to operate entirely offline. Internet connectivity is only required for optional Google Drive backup and restore.
